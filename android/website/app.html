🤖 Maya AI
Your Personal Learning Companion
Offline AI Education • Privacy First • Always Available
Maya AI brings the power of artificial intelligence to every student, regardless of internet connectivity. Designed for learners aged 10-18, Maya AI runs completely on your device, providing instant answers, explanations, and learning support without requiring constant internet access or compromising your privacy.

🎯 Why Maya AI?

In many parts of the world, students face a critical challenge: limited or unreliable internet access. Maya AI bridges this digital divide by bringing advanced AI capabilities directly to your device. Download once, learn forever.
